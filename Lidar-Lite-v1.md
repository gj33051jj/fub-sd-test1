Mapping for the Fubarino Mini: SPI

Pin | Label | Fubarino Mini | Uno
---- | ---- | ---- | ----
1 | CD | 23 | CardDetect -> any digital pin
2 | < CS | 30 | 10
3 | < DI | 29 | 11
4 | > DO | 27 | 12
5 | < CLK |4 | 13 
6 | GND | GND | GND
7 | 3v| 3.3v | 3.3v
8 | 5v |Not connected | Not connected
